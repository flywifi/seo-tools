# audience-research artifact types

## Audience profile
A mapping of audience signals to the five-persona model. Required elements: primary_persona (one of the five), secondary_personas (list), fit_rationale (one sentence per persona, grounded in content signals), adaptation_notes (budget, skill level, and tenure considerations per persona), data_source (provided/inferred/niche-typical-default), and a govern-artifact gate result. Inferred mappings are labeled "inferred from topic signals"; niche-typical defaults are labeled as defaults, not measured data.

## Persona map
A single-topic persona assignment. Required elements: primary_persona, secondary_personas (list, may be empty), fit_rationale, adaptation_notes, and confidence (high/medium/low reflecting signal strength).

## Comment intelligence summary
Audience signals extracted from comment data. Required elements: injection_scan_result (from ingest-route), comment themes list, sentiment distribution (positive/neutral/negative), questions asked by viewers (list), and persona fit observations.
