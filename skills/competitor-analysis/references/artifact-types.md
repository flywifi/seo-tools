# competitor-analysis artifact types

## Competitive report
A research report on competitors and content gaps in the moody/vintage home decor and DIY niche. Required elements: competitors list (each with name, url_if_found, scale [small/medium/large], content_angle; unverified fields labeled [unverified]), overserved topics list, underserved topics list, keyword gaps list, differentiation_summary, confidence (reflecting retrieval quality), retrieval_gaps list, and a govern-artifact gate result.

## Competitor scan
A raw competitor list for one topic and platform. Required elements: list of up to count competitors (each: name [unverified], url_if_found or null, scale_tier [estimated], content_angle, gap_or_differentiation), gap_summary, and confidence.

## Gap analysis
Overserved vs. underserved angles on a topic. Required elements: overserved_angles (list), underserved_angles (list), most actionable gap sentence, evidence source (web retrieval or niche knowledge), confidence level.
