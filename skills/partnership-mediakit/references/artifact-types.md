# partnership-mediakit artifact types

## Media kit package
A complete brand partnership outreach set. Required elements: pitch paragraph (150 to 250 words, brand-specific, with subject line options and personalization notes), media kit sections (each with title, body, data_source label, and placeholders_to_fill), rate card (each line item with format, rate_or_range, source label [personal_rate/benchmark_range], notes), disclaimer (when benchmark rates are used), and a govern-artifact gate result.

## Pitch paragraph
One personalized outreach paragraph. Required elements: pitch_paragraph (150 to 250 words), subject_line_options (exactly 3), personalization_notes (at least one item to verify before sending), and an FTC note if content is sponsored.

## Rate card
A formatted rate card for a brand proposal. Required elements: list of formats with rate_or_range, source label (personal_rate or benchmark_range), notes, disclaimer (when benchmark_range is used for any item), and recommended_negotiation_floor.

## Media kit section
One section of a brand-facing media kit. Required elements: section_title, section_body (markdown), data_source (real/benchmark/placeholder), and placeholders_to_fill (list of fields needing real data before the section is shared externally).
