# content-strategy artifact types

## Idea cluster
A set of related ideas around one pillar or season. Required elements: each idea has a working title,
pillar, format, persona served, hook angle, scale, and a follow-on seed; the cluster names which
ideas depend on a verified trend and carries any retrieval gaps.

## Pillar plan
A pillar by pillar view of what to make next. Required elements: a healthy mix of growth, depth, and
community roles (`shared/audience-engine.md`), with seasonal timing where relevant.

## Competitive positioning note
How the creator stands out in the home decor space. Required elements: the market gap named, the
aesthetic difference, and at least one verified competitor signal (cited per
`protocols/research-citation.md`), never invented.
