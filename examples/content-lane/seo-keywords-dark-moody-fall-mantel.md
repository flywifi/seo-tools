## Seasonal timing summary

**Fall peak window:** September 15 to October 20

- **YouTube publish by:** September 1 (gives the algorithm 2 to 3 weeks to index before peak search)
- **Pinterest pins live by:** August 15 (pins take 2 to 6 weeks to compound; early pins rank higher during peak)
- **Shorts publish:** can go up any time in the 4 weeks before peak; Shorts distribute by behavior not publication date
- **Adjacent content note:** "vintage christmas decor" and holiday keywords are off-season for fall. Do not mix fall and holiday in the same video title; Pinterest's algorithm will misclassify the seasonal intent.
